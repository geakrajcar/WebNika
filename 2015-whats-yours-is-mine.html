what's yours is mine

Assuming the idea that every social exchange involves a chemical reaction among us, what are we altered by and what becomes altered?

choreography


The starting exploration for the duet “I am not myself”.

A chemical change is a process where one or more substances are altered into one or more new and different substances. While a physical change can often be reversed, a chemical change typically cannot be, except through more chemical reactions. When a chemical change occurs, there is also a change in the energy of the system.
