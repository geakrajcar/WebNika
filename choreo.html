choreo

Move your body so that you can move the borders of your creativity. Listen to it, explore it's limits and possibilities.
Express more. Play more. Make mistakes. Ask yourself why.
Read. Sit in the sun, relax. Notice the blue sky. Breathe.
Learn to move safely and protect your body from unnecessary injuries.
Find where your body starts and whether it ends. Or not.
Switch constantly between reactions and actions. They both have a learning purpose.
What inspires you? What moves you?
