performance

Through movement, or lack of it, I expand the borders of my creativity. In stillness I search for patience, interconnectedness of movement and mind, the beginning of intention. Being calm, listening to it.
Moving to express the subtle, invisible parts of myself. Breathing.
I move to discover if there is an end to my body. While switching between actions and reactions I learn more about what triggers me, in which way. Dancing, moving, it compliments my curiosity.
