Dance, Arian!

How can the body keep transforming into a series of fairytale-like creatures?

choreography


A contemporary dance theater performance which explored the possibilities of movement through the technique of Breakdancing.

Arian is reshaped- through exploration of broken down shapes which come to life, his and the perspective of the audience shifted to explore the possibilities of storytelling.
